# Sales Performance & Product Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard was created to analyze sales performance, product trends, shipping preferences, and regional sales distribution using the Superstore dataset.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support decision-making and performance analysis.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization
* Business Analytics

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales: 168.45K
* Total Profit: 14.63K
* Total Orders: 793
* Profit Margin: 8.69%

---

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Analysis

* Sales by Region
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales by Category

### Product Analysis

* Top 10 Products by Sales

### Shipping Analysis

* Sales by Ship Mode

### Interactive Filters

* Region Filter
* City Filter

---

## Key Insights

* East region generated the highest sales among all regions.
* October recorded the highest sales performance.
* Technology dominated sales across most regions, while Furniture led sales in the West region.
* Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping mode.
* Canon ImageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier was the highest revenue-generating product.

---

## Business Value

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Identify high-performing regions.
* Track monthly sales performance.
* Understand customer shipping preferences.
* Analyze category-wise sales trends.
* Monitor top revenue-generating products.
